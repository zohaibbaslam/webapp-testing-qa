Bug ID: BUG-003
Title: Cart badge/count may not update instantly after Reset App State until navigation/refresh
Environment: Windows 11, Chrome 144.0.7559.133
Severity: Minor
Priority: Low
Module: Cart / App State
Status: Open

## Preconditions
1. User is logged in as `standard_user`
2. At least one product is added to the cart and cart badge shows a count

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Add 1â€“2 products to the cart and confirm cart badge shows the count
2. Click the menu icon
3. Click "Reset App State"
4. Observe the cart badge/count immediately
5. Navigate to another page (e.g., Cart or Products) or refresh, and observe again

## Expected Result
Cart badge/count should clear immediately after resetting the app state.

## Actual Result
Cart badge/count may remain visible temporarily and clears only after navigation or refresh.

## Evidence
Screenshot: `evidence/screenshots/BUG-003.png`

## Impact
User receives inconsistent visual feedback about whether the cart is cleared.

## Notes
If the badge clears immediately on your machine, mark this as **Not Reproducible** and do not count it as a bug.
